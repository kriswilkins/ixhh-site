Our Mission

At IX Hostage House, we breathe life into history through the timeless practice of ancient crafts and community celebration. Our medieval village serves as a living workshop where traditional skills are preserved, practiced, and passed forward by dedicated artisans who find joy in creating with their hands as our ancestors once did. We are committed to cultivating an inclusive space where knowledge flows freely between masters and apprentices, where the forgotten arts of leatherwork, woodturning, brewing, textile creation, and countless other crafts flourish under careful stewardship. Through immersive experiences, hands-on classes, and festive gatherings within our authentic setting, we seek to honor the ingenuity of medieval craftspeople while building a modern community bound together by shared curiosity, creative expression, and the enduring human desire to make something beautiful and lasting with the distinctive spirit of IX Hostage House.
